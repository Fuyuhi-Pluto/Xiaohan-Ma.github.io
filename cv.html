Research Experience

Graduate Research Assistant

Boston University, Center for Space Physics

2020 - Present

PI: Prof. Merav Opher

  • Developed and executed 3D MHD simulations of plasma instabilities in the heliosheath using BATS-R-US code
  • Discovered and confirmed the presence of Kelvin-Helmholtz instability in the heliosheath
  • Modeled the quasi-periodic coupling and decoupling of RT and KH instabilities
